SAMSUNG

Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd. is a South Korean multinational electronics company best known for its consumer electronics, including smartphones, televisions, and home appliances. It is a major manufacturer of semiconductors, display panels, and memory chips. Samsung's Galaxy line of smartphones and tablets is among its flagship products. As of 2024, Samsung is one of the largest technology companies in the world by revenue and plays a pivotal role in the global electronics industry.
